A simple 10-minute practice loop
If your speaking habit has felt inconsistent, start here:
1. Answer one IELTS-style prompt.
2. Read the transcript and mark one weak area.
3. Retry the same prompt with one stronger reason and one clearer example.
That loop is small enough to repeat and strong enough to actually move fluency, structure, and confidence.
